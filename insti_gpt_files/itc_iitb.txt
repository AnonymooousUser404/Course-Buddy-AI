Hostel Affairs Council at IIT Bombay

Overview

The Hostel Affairs Council at IIT Bombay aims to enhance the quality of
student life on campus by managing various aspects such as
infrastructure, security, medical facilities, internet access,
accommodation, housekeeping, sustainability, and food services.

Key Responsibilities

-   Infrastructure & Development:** Maintaining and developing hostel
    facilities.
-   Security Measures: Implementing campus-wide safety procedures.
-   Medical Services: Managing hospital facilities for students and
    staff.
-   Internet & IT Support: Providing reliable connectivity and
    technical assistance.
-   Accommodation: Offering comfortable student housing.
-   Housekeeping: Ensuring cleanliness in hostels.
-   Sustainability Practices: Encouraging eco-friendly habits and
    initiatives.
-   Food Services: Managing campus canteens and food outlets.

Committees & Teams:

Hygiene Committee

-   Focus: Maintaining hygiene in campus food outlets.
-   Activities: Performing regular inspections and surprise visits.

Sustainability Cell

-   Mission: Promote sustainability awareness and integrate green
    technologies.
-   Collaborative Efforts: Work closely with other campus departments
    for sustainable development.
-   Challenges Addressed: Energy efficiency, local food sourcing, and
    waste reduction.

Gender Equality & Anti-Harassment Cell

-   Objectives: Prevent sexual harassment, ensure gender inclusiveness,
    and address related concerns.
-   Structure: Utilizes an Internal Complaints Committee.

International Relations

-   Tasks: Facilitate cross-cultural exchanges, coordinate academic
    collaborations, manage MOUs, and update the IR website.

IIT Bombay prioritizes safety, wellness, and convenient living
arrangements for both students and staff. With effective security
measures, diversified food choices, holistic healthcare provisions, and

learning atmosphere.
